Winning City Worldwide

Winning Life Bible Church

The Church

Winning Life Bible Church is a vibrant, spirit-filled church dedicated to raising disciples and transforming lives through the undiluted word of God. Founded on truth, love, and purpose, we exist to help individuals discover their God-ordained destiny and walk in victory daily. Our services are marked by passionate worship, impactful teachings, and a strong sense of community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to preach the gospel of Christ to all nations, build lives through the word, and equip believers to become agents of change in their generation. We are committed to spreading the message of hope, healing, and restoration while nurturing spiritual growth and leadership among our members.

Our Vision

To be a global church with a local impact—raising kingdom-minded believers who live victoriously, influence culture, and expand the dominion of Christ in every sphere of life. Our vision is to raise a generation of people empowered to succeed in life and fulfill divine purpose.

Rev Solomon

    

Rev. Solomon B. Divine is the visionary leader and senior pastor of Winning Life Bible Church. With a heart full of compassion and a mandate to raise end-time generals, he ministers with a deep prophetic grace and practical wisdom. His teachings challenge the status quo and inspire men and women to live purpose-driven lives rooted in God’s word.

The Mandate

The mandate of Winning Life Bible Church is “Raising Champions, Changing Lives.” We are called to break yokes, build lives, and ignite revival in our generation. Our church is not just a place of worship but a center for transformation where lives are molded, and destinies are fulfilled.
