WINNING CITY WORLDWIDE WINNING LIFE BIBLE CHURCH

The Church

Winning Life Bible Church is a vibrant and growing community of believers committed to spreading the gospel, building strong disciples, and impacting lives globally with the love of Christ.

Our Mission

To reach the lost, nurture the saved, and empower individuals to walk in the fullness of God’s purpose through teaching, fellowship, and community service.

Our Vision

To become a global network of believers who demonstrate God’s kingdom values in every sphere of life, transforming communities with love and integrity.

Rev S.G Erochukwu (General Overseer)

Rev. S.G Erochukwu is a passionate servant of God, dedicated to spiritual growth, discipleship, and equipping the body of Christ for kingdom impact.

The Mandate

To preach the gospel of Jesus Christ boldly, raise leaders, and establish communities that reflect God’s love and righteousness in every nation.
